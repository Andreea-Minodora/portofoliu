How to get to Radisson Blu Aurum Hotel, Brasov

From Brasov International Airport (GHV)

By car or taxi:

At the airport, take Strada Aeroportului Brasov to Strada Albinelor/DJ103C. Once on the DJ103, head towards Strada Lungă and later take Bulevardul Eroilor/DN1E in Brasov. The journey is about 9 kilometers long and takes approximately 15 minutes, depending on traffic.

By bus:

At the airport, take the A1 towards the city center and hop off at the Livada Poștei stop. The hotel is just 500 meters away. The journey is about 32 minutes long, depending on traffic.

From Henri Coanda Bucharest International Airport

By train:

At Henri Coandă Bucharest Airport, catch the train to North Railway Station. At North Railway Station, change to the direct line to Brasov Railway Station. At Brasov Railway Station, a bus will then take you to Livada Poștei bus station. Or, you can take a taxi from Brasov Railway Station to the hotel in 10 minutes.

By bus:

A shuttle bus will take you to Livada Poștei Bus Station in Brasov from the main airport near Bucharest. The journey takes approximately 2 hours and 40 minutes. Once at the bus station, you can reach the hotel on foot in 5 minutes.

By car:

Driving from Henri Coandă International Airport to the hotel takes approximately 2 hours and 30 minutes.

From Livada Poștei bus stop

By foot:

You can reach the hotel from the bus stop in about 5 minutes on foot.
